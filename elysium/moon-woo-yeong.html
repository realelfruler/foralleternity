Moon Woo-yeong (文 宇靈) is the second son of Moon Seong-jin (文 盛眞) and Moon Seo-yoon (文 瑞潤). His mother is the older sister of Myung Ji-hyun (明 智賢), sei-kyōshi of the Choseungdal Temple in Byeoljari. Woo-yeong inherited the Myung family's aiblity to see wolgwang, similar to the Upper Lands' conception of auras.

At age , Woo-yeong had the eldest daughter of the Namu family promised to him in marriage. At this point, Namu Ha-eun moved into Byeol Miya Palace, where the two were raised side-by-side together.

In 2019, when Woo-yeong was 15, his family was driving back from their vacation home by the Anpu Lakes in the southern part of Elysium. Low visibility from a strong rainstorm eventually led to the carriage crashing into another carriage coming from the opposite direction, killing Seong-jin on impact. His older sister, Moon Seong-min (文 瑞潤) died several days later, and Woo-yeong lost his left leg.

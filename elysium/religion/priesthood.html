Darui Saenggi priests have two hierarchies, the Seon (선; Common: Virtue) ranks and Gyosa (교사; Common: Teacher). To become a priest, one must go through the training at an official training center, called a susaeunsincheo (수사은신처). Going up in the ranks is mainly dependent on how many years a person has been in training at the susaeunsincheo. For Seonsang ranks, one must also achieve a certain level of formal education.

The first two Seon ranks are done with the explicit goal of becoming a seonsangnim. Once this rank has been achieved, one has the option to further continue their training and education, or to finish their training and stay at that rank. The transition from these two novice ranks is marked by the changing of robes from black to the classic bright blue robes of Darui Saenggi monks and nuns.

Each state in Elysium has a head temple, lead by a myeong-in. Other temples within each state are lead by tag-wolhan seonsangnim, who answer to their state's myeong-in. Those who have achieved a Gyosa rank are responsible for training incoming monks, while the myeong-in is in charge of supervising this training.

Myung Byeol-bit (明 별빛)

The House of Myung (also called the Myung family; 明 선조) has been one of the most powerful families in Elysium since its founding in year zero of the Unseelie lunar calendar (2333 BCE). While the historical facts of their rise to power are not known, tradition teaches that the first member of the Myung family, Myung Byeol-bit (明 별빛) was born on the 10th day of Tokkidal, the second month in the lunar year.

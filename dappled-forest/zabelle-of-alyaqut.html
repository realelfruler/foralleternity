Zabelle of Alyaqut

Back

Birthday: 27 January 1996

Pronouns: she/her

Zabelle ó Broin was born on the island nation of Ghaniun Bial'alwan, to Seelie parents of unknown heritage. She was surrendered anonymously to an orphanage in the capitol of Alyaqut when she was only a couple days old, and she lived there for the first year of her life.

One of the longest-working staff members, Anton is the only human within Caisbaile’s walls. He doesn’t talk much about where he’s from, though it’s known one of his flower beds if filled with his wife’s favorite, forget-me-nots. He’s peaceful and welcoming to new hires, and will always listen to another’s concerns. Anton watched over Calla when she was young and seems more loyal to her than to Queen Chrysanthemum.

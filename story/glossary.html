Acadamh Leighis Ríoga: The Royal Medical Academy, located in County Uamh, Dappled Forest. Because of immigration laws in the country, An T-Acadamh only admits Seelie fae living in the Dappled Forest, Finapor, and The Highlands.

Akeel Akedimeya: The Crown Academy, located in the Naranthian capitol of Kolona. Open to all students.

Archon: Naranthian for "ruler" or "lord," and used for most nobility. Followed either by surname or the region the noble rules over.

Bergeworten: Based off of German. Comes from the Glockenblume Mountains, especially The Highlands.

Blutend Herz: The official religion of The Highlands.

Ceathodann: The official religion of both the Dappled Forest and Finapor. The two countries' religions have slight differences in practice, and are considered to be separate denominations.

Coillecànan: Based off of Scottish. Comes from County Láimh in the Dappled Forest, with most of the language's speakers coming from there. It is one of the two official languages of the kingdom, with speakers in other counties, as well as in Ghaniun Bil'alwan.

Common: Based off of English. Most of the characters in For All Eternity speak this. It is more common amongst the upper class to speak common, with people in lower classes having little to no need to speak an extra language.

Elysian: Based off of Korean. Official language of Elysium, and spoken by majority of the Unseelie. The language is virtually unknown in the Upper Kingdoms.

Finaporean: Based off of French. Official language of Finapor, and commonly spoken by fae in the Dappled Forest, Ghaniun Bil'alwan, and The Highlands. Some elves know it, but those instances are rare (See Lapinois).

Foraoisteanga: Based off of Irish. One of the two official languages of the Dappled Forest, with majority of the citizens speaking it. It is also spoken in Finapor, Ghaniun Bil'alwan, and The Highlands.

Gaghtnik: Based off of Eastern Armenian. Spoken by the fae minority in Ghaniun Bil'alwan. Not commonly spoken on the mainland, though there are small communities who speak it in La Costa de Melograno.

Honoria/us: Naranthian for "honored", used primarily when speaking of royals, but also when speaking about some other notable people. May be shortened and used without the last name, with appropriate gendered ending.

Kalimat 'Ugni: Based off of Arabic. Spoken primarily by sirens and the elven minority in Ghaniun Bil'alwan. Spoken in some places in the southern part of La Costa de Melograno.

Lapinois: Based off of Quebecois. Spoken primarily in the Outer Lands by non-Seelie fae, especially púca.

Melogranian: Based off of Italian. One of the two main languages in La Costa de Melograno, spoken by a majority of the population. Also spoken in Ghaniun Bil'alwan and Naranthia, especially the region of Thalassia.

Motukohanian: Based off of Hawaiian. One of the two main languages in La Costa de Melograno.

Naranthian: Based off of Amharic. One of the two main languages in Naranthia. Most speakers live in or near the capitol of Kolona, but the language is also spoken in other parts of the country, as well as in La Costa de Melograno and Ghaniun Bil'alwan.

Tafod: Based off of Welsh. Spoken primarily by humans living in the eastern part of the Outer Lands, as well as humans living within the Dappled Forest and The Highlands.

Thalassian: Based off of Greek. Spoken primarily in Thalassia, a southern region in Naranthia, as well as in La Costa De Melograno, Ghaniun Bil'alwan, and the Fulican Archipelago. Most speakers are elves, but a large portion are also sirens.

Yengus Ker Kinät Akedimeya: The Royal Fiber Arts Academy of Naranthia. Located in the capitol, Kolona, and was attended by Royal Seamstress Fadia Kaur.

For All Eternity

For All Eternity (FAE) takes place in the Magick Realm, a complicated, storied world managed and created by three friends. The main story follows Prince Knox of Naranthia and Captain Fechin Dune of the Dappled Forest as they grapple with cultural differences, politics, the growing divide between their two countries, and the anger between their people.
